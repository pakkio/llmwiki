## Overview

MindStudio (by GoMeta, Inc.) is a no-code platform for building [[AI agent|AI agents]] and workflows. It can wrap a [[Personal Knowledge Base]] (such as [[Karpathy's LLM Wiki]]) in a shareable agent that anyone on a team can query through a clean web interface, without needing to use a terminal or [[Claude Code]] directly.

## How It Complements an LLM Wiki

For individual workflows — just the user and local files — [[Claude Code]] direct access is the simpler approach. For team-facing or scaled knowledge bases, MindStudio handles the infrastructure:

- Accepts natural language questions through a custom UI
- Searches wiki files (synced to [[Google Drive]], [[Notion]], or any connected storage)
- Returns cited, grounded answers
- Logs queries to show what people are looking for — and what the wiki is missing

## Capabilities

- Access to 200+ [[Large Language Model|LLMs]] (Claude, GPT, Gemini, etc.) out of the box
- 1,000+ pre-built integrations ([[Slack]], email, project management tools)
- No-code agent and workflow builder
- Faster path to team deployment than standing up a custom [[Retrieval-Augmented Generation|RAG]] pipeline
